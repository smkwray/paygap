4. Oaxaca Decomposition Trend

Oaxaca: Explained vs Unexplained Share (ACS)

After 2019, the unexplained share rises sharply (74% → 88-94%) while the total gap stays stable. This reflects a collapse in the explained component, not an explosion in the total gap.

5. ACS Specification Ladder (2023)

Sequential Control Blocks (ACS 2023)

The M2→M3 step (adding occupation and industry) drives the largest gap reduction: ~8 percentage points. Family controls (M5) add only 0.06 pp.
