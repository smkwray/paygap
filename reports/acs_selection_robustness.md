# ACS Selection Robustness

This note summarizes the ACS employment-selection robustness layer.

## Headline pattern (S2 block)
- Years covered: 2015-2023
- Mean female employment probability effect: -8.76 percentage points
- Mean combined expected annual earnings gap: 38.48%
- Mean observed total annual earnings gap: 36.55%
- Mean observed worker hourly wage gap: 18.83%
- Mean IPW worker hourly wage gap: 19.73%

## Interpretation
- This is a robustness layer for labor-force selection, not a full structural selection correction.
- The worker-only hourly wage gap and the combined expected annual-earnings gap answer different questions.
- The IPW worker wage gap should be read as a sensitivity check on worker-only estimates, not as the main estimand.
